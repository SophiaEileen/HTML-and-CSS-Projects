"There are over 500 species of shark and they have been around for over 400 million years! Sharks can vary dramatically in size. For instance, the Dwarf Lantern Shark can be about the size of a human hand while whale sharks can reach up to 12 metres long. Sharks also have a sixth sense - they can sense electrical fields emitted by animals in surrounding waters."
